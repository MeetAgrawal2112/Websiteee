Alumni

Alumni are assets for each institute of academic learning. We take pride in our alumni and in their achievements. Two batches of engineering (B.Tech CSE/ECE) have graduated from IIIT Pune so far since its inception in 2016. We are proud to say that our alumni have shown exemplary performance both in terms of placements and higher studies.  Our beloved alumni are rapidly spreading among different sectors and are taking the name of the institute forward with them.

 

Kindly write to us at admin@iiitp.ac.in to get any specific information or connect with any of our alumni. You may also find them on their LinkedIn Pages.
