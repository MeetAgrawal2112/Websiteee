Q-Riocity
Official Quizzing Society of IIIT Pune

Q-Riocity is the Quizzing Society of the Indian Institute of Information Technology Pune. It is a student community built around curiosity, quick thinking, observation, teamwork, and the joy of learning beyond the classroom.

The club brings together students who enjoy exploring the world through quizzes, discussions, games, and interactive activities across a wide range of topics including history, geography, current affairs, global culture, pop-culture, sports, literature, cinema, anime, defence, science, technology, space, languages, flags, maps, internet culture, and more.

About the Club

Q-Riocity is more than a quiz club. It is a close-knit community where students can learn, host, participate, and have fun with any topic they are passionate about.

Whether someone is interested in world history, sports trivia, mythology, military vehicles, cinema, anime, memes, geography, linguistics, technology, or obscure science facts, Q-Riocity gives them a platform to explore and share that curiosity.

Our Activities

Freshers Quiz

At the beginning of the academic session, Q-Riocity organizes a Freshers Quiz to introduce first-year students to the quizzing culture of IIIT Pune.

Theme-Based Quiz Sessions

Q-Riocity regularly conducts quiz sessions across a wide variety of themes including anime, sports, geography, science, technology, arts, literature, pop culture, history, cinema, and current affairs.

Interactive Learning and Games

The club also conducts interactive learning-based games such as GeoGuessr sessions, Skribbl sessions, Wikipedia Racing, Family-Feud inspired activities, and other creative quiz formats.

Quiz Workshops and Club Meets

Regular club meets are conducted for planning, brainstorming, and coordination including quiz themes, question framing, round design, recruitment, onboarding, and internal coordination.

Major Events

Cognition: Seikai Showdown

Cognition: Seikai Showdown was one of the major quiz events conducted by Q-Riocity during the academic year.

The event included multiple-choice rounds, visual identification, map-based clues, buzzer rounds, common-link questions, country silhouette identification, and language recognition formats.

Sakura Survey

Sakura Survey was a Family Feud-style interactive event organized by Q-Riocity where participants guessed the most common responses given by students.

Community and Learning

Q-Riocity believes that learning becomes more meaningful when it is shared.

The club provides a welcoming environment where students can bring their own interests, learn from others, and participate without needing to be experts.
