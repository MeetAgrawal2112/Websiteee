Biography & Research

Biography

Dr. Sanga Chaki Dr. Sanga Chaki is currently working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Pune, Maharashtra. Prior to that she has worked as an Assistant Professor in the department of Computer Science and Engineering, ITER, Siksha O Anusandhan, Bhubaneswar. She received her Ph.D. degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur in 2023. Dr. Chaki completed her M.E. and B.E. in Information Technology (IT) from Bengal Engineering and Science University Shibpur (IIEST), and West Bengal University of Technology in 2012 and 2010 respectively.

Dr. Sanga's areas of interest include Explainable Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Affective Computing, Sound and Music Computing, and Trustworthy AI.
