Welcome to IIIT Pune!

Welcome to Indian Institute of Information Technology Pune, a center of excellence dedicated to innovation, research, and quality technical education. Guided by strong values and ethical principles, IIIT Pune nurtures skilled professionals and future leaders in Information Technology and allied fields. The institute fosters creativity, entrepreneurship, and collaborative learning while promoting inclusivity, integrity, and social responsibility. With a commitment to advanced research and Indian knowledge systems, IIIT Pune strives to create meaningful impact for society and the global community.
